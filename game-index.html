About Me

Miranda is a web designer and prop artist. In her spare time, she likes to draw and play video games. Her favorite kind of games are the ones you enjoy with others because cooperative experiences are the most fun in her opinion. She is majoring in web design and game design at Purdue University. In regards to her majors, Miranda wants to create art and games that people can enjoy for a long time.
